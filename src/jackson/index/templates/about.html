{% translate "Comprehensive Eye Examinations" %}

{% blocktrans %} Regular eye exams are a cornerstone of maintaining vibrant, healthy eyes, essential for both adults and children. Some conditions progress silently, causing no discomfort or vision impairment. Early detection is our focus, reducing risks and broadening treatment choices. Our comprehensive exams encompass a range of assessments, including:

  • Optical Coherence Tomography
  • Visual Field Test
  • Fundus Photo Exam
  • B-Scan
  • Pachymetry
  • Dilation
  • Exterior Photos of the Eye
  • Slit lamp examination
  • Refraction for glasses and contact lenses
  • Glaucoma pressure testing
  • Diabetic evaluations
  • Series of advanced computerized tests for a comprehensive evaluation of eye health.
